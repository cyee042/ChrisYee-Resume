Since the onset of civilization, laws and policies have been established in hopes of nourishing a productive and orderly society. However, where is the boundary and distinction between law and infringment upon our God-given rights. My passion and goal is to provide guidance for those navigating the complex legal system of this nation. As someone who wants to specialize in different fields of law, my clients will come from all walks of life, ranging from undocumented persons to CEOs and politicians. I want to help my clients to better understand their rights and ensure the due process of law in all legal matters.

I understand that the legal system of this nation is multifaceted and intensive with hundreds upon thousands of laws, policies, and codes that impact our rights and everyday activities. As such, I plan to be educated in numerous fields of law, including environmental, constitutional, and corporate law, and want to specialize in family and immigration law. I understand that no single approach to working with clients is the most effective and am open to working with clients virtually as well as in person. Furthermore, I understand the diverse range of skills that my profession requires as I have been educated and trained in child developmental psychology, communications, and public speaking. I am also fluent in both Spanish and Cantonese.

My education background includes a high school diploma and I am currently a first year studying political science at the Univeristy of Califonria Riverside.
